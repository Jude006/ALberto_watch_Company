Our Technology

Innovations That Define Precision

At Alberto Watch Company, we embrace cutting-edge technology to bring you timepieces
of unparalleled quality and precision.

Swiss Movement

Our watches are equipped with precise Swiss movements, ensuring exceptional accuracy and longevity in every tick. Swiss movements are renowned for their engineering excellence, providing reliable timekeeping that you can depend on, year after year.

Sapphire Crystal

We use sapphire crystal glass for its superior scratch resistance and clarity, protecting the watch face from daily wear and tear. Sapphire crystal is second only to diamond in hardness, ensuring that your watch maintains its pristine look even in the harshest conditions.

Water Resistance

Our advanced sealing technology ensures robust water resistance, allowing you to wear your watch with confidence in any environment. Whether you're swimming, showering, or caught in the rain, our watches are designed to withstand water exposure and keep ticking accurately.

Luminous Hands

Featuring state-of-the-art luminous hands, our watches provide optimal visibility in low-light conditions, keeping you on time day or night. The luminous material absorbs light during the day and glows brightly at night, ensuring that you can always read the time, no matter the lighting.

Automatic Winding

Our automatic winding mechanism harnesses the kinetic energy from your wrist's movements to keep the watch running. This technology eliminates the need for manual winding or battery changes, offering a seamless and environmentally friendly timekeeping experience.

Ceramic Bezel

The ceramic bezel on our watches is not only aesthetically pleasing but also highly durable. Resistant to scratches and fading, the ceramic bezel retains its luster and color even after years of use, ensuring that your watch looks as good as new for a long time.
